Engr. Jamsher Bhanbhro is currently a PhD Researcher at the University of Calabria (DIMES), working on efficient, secure, and sustainable federated learning under the FAIR project. He received his ME in Computer and Information Engineering from MUET, Jamshoro (2023), and BE in Computer Systems Engineering from MUET (2020). From September 2024 to September 2025 he was a Visiting Researcher at the University of Miami, working with Prof. Mingzhe Chen. He has served as Teaching Assistant at MUET (2021–2022) and as a contract-based Lecturer in the Department of Computer Systems Engineering, MUET, for three months before starting his PhD. He works as a Freelance Software and ML Developer on Fiverr and Upwork (Level Two Seller, Top-Rated) since 2019.

Research Interest:

  • Federated learning: efficiency, security, and sustainability
  • Privacy-preserving and secure distributed learning
  • Machine learning and deep learning for signal processing and emotion recognition
  • Sustainable AI and energy-aware federated learning
